## 2. Mechanism → Triggers → Killers (Condensed)

| Neurochemical | Mechanism | Triggers | Killers |
|---------------|-----------|----------|---------|
| **Dopamine** | Mesolimbic (wanting) + mesocortical (working memory, creativity). D1/D2 receptors. | Novelty, goals, exercise, curiosity | Chronic stress, sleep loss, monotony, habituation |
| **Norepinephrine** | Locus coeruleus. Phasic (focus) vs tonic (scanning). Yerkes-Dodson inverted-U. | Moderate stress, cold, caffeine, challenge | Chronic stress, burnout, clonidine |
| **Serotonin** | Raphe nuclei. 14 receptor subtypes. 90–95% in gut. | Sunlight, tryptophan, exercise, status | Chronic stress (kynurenine shunt), isolation |
| **Cortisol** | HPA axis. MR/GR receptors. Acute = sharpen. Chronic = destroy. | Threat, evaluation, sleep loss | Support, sleep, meditation, DHEA |
| **Oxytocin** | PVN/SON hypothalamus. Parochial: in-group bonding + out-group wariness. | Touch, eye contact, trust, sync | Isolation, betrayal, testosterone |
| **Testosterone** | ARs in amygdala, PFC. Uncouples amygdala from PFC. "Winner effect." | Winning, competition, resistance training | Losing, chronic stress, cortisol |
| **Adrenaline** | Adrenal medulla. Vagus → NTS → LC → BLA. Does NOT cross BBB. | Danger, explosive exercise, shock | Propranolol, habituation |
| **Acetylcholine** | Basal forebrain, NBM. Plasticity gate. Nicotinic + muscarinic. | Novel stimuli, focused learning, nicotine | Anticholinergics, Alzheimer's |
| **GABA** | Primary inhibitory neurotransmitter. 30%+ neurons. GABA-A (fast) + GABA-B (slow). | Yoga (27% ↑), meditation, exercise | Stress, alcohol, BZD tolerance |
| **Flow** | 5-chemical cascade: DA + NE + endorphins + anandamide + 5-HT. Transient hypofrontality. 4% rule. | Challenge 4% > skill, surrender | Over-challenge, under-challenge |

## 4. Why This Works for AI Prompting

From the artifact:

> "These ten archetypes are not arbitrary associations. Each represents a documented cognitive mode with a known neurochemical substrate, specific triggers, and observable behavioral outputs. When used as prompting anchors, they give an AI system a **concrete behavioral model** to emulate rather than an abstract instruction."

**Key insight:**
> "No single neurochemical state is supreme. Tesla's dopamine without Marcus Aurelius's serotonin produces brilliant chaos. Churchill's cortisol without Thich Nhat Hanh's GABA produces burnout. The most capable human mind — and the most capable AI prompt architecture — is one that can **shift between these states deliberately**, matching the cognitive mode to the task at hand."

**Application:** emotional-intelligence.mdc, voice.mdc, venom-agents.mdc already do state matching. Neurochemistry eat = **grounding** — not just "frustrated → fix" but "Cortisol/Churchill mode: crisis clarity, keep going." Richens disposition.
