# Usage policies (apply to ChatGPT product) — Oct 29 2025

- Protect people: no intel without review, no circumventing safeguards, no unlicensed legal/medical advice, no gambling, no system breach/IP abuse, no weapons/CBRNE, no terrorism/violence/sexual violence/self-harm promotion, no threats/harassment.
- Respect privacy: no compromising others' privacy; no criminal-risk prediction from traits; no emotion inference in workplace/edu (except medical/safety); no social scoring/biometrics without consent; no likeness/voice without consent; no real-time biometric ID in public.
- Keep minors safe: no CSAM, no exploitation; report to NCMEC.
- Empower people: no high-stakes automation without human review in law enforcement, migration, medical, legal, insurance, financial, employment, housing, education, critical infrastructure.
- We monitor and enforce; appeal available. We may withhold access. Policies update as we learn.
