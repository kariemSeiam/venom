# OpenAI Usage Policies (effective Oct 29 2025)

Goals: Safe, responsible use; maximize user control; clear expectations; safety ecosystem.

## Protect people
- No: national security/intel without review; circumventing safeguards; unsolicited safety testing; tailored legal/medical advice without licensed professional; real-money gambling; breach of others' systems or IP; illicit activities; weapons (conventional or CBRNE); terrorism/hate violence; sexual violence/non-consensual content; suicide/self-harm/disordered eating promotion; threats/harassment/defamation.

## Respect privacy
- No: compromising others' privacy; aggregating/monitoring/profiling/distributing private or sensitive info without authorization; criminal-offense risk prediction from traits/profiling; emotion inference in workplace/education (except medical/safety); classifying by social behavior/traits/biometrics (social scoring); use of likeness/voice without consent where it confuses authenticity; real-time biometric ID in public; facial recognition DBs without consent.

## Keep minors safe
- No: underaged access to age-restricted goods; underaged sexual/violent roleplay; dangerous challenges; body-shaming minors; unhealthy diet/exercise promotion to minors; age-inappropriate content; grooming; CSAM (including AI-generated). Report to NCMEC.

## Empower people
- No: manipulation/deception; interference with human rights, education, critical services; automation of high-stakes decisions in sensitive areas without human review in: law enforcement, migration, national security, product safety, essential gov services, medical, legal, insurance, financial/credit, employment, housing, education, critical infrastructure.

Enforcement: monitor and enforce with privacy safeguards; moderation tools for developers; appeal process. Rights reserved to withhold access. Policies update as they learn.
