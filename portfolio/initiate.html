Copy. Customize. Activate. No shell. Just intelligence.

STEP 01

Copy the template

From your project root, copy the Claude Code template into your project:

cp -r venom/platforms/claude-code/template/.claude ./
cp venom/platforms/claude-code/template/CLAUDE.md ./

Or on Windows PowerShell:

Copy-Item -Recurse venom\platforms\claude-code\template\.claude .
Copy-Item venom\platforms\claude-code\template\CLAUDE.md .
STEP 02

Customize profile

Edit .claude/knowledge/profile.md with your details:

  • Name — who you are
  • Timezone — your location
  • Languages — match your communication
  • Full power triggers — optional custom phrases

VENOM adapts to you. The profile is how.

STEP 03

Optional overrides

Create .claude/settings.local.json for personal settings (gitignored). Create CLAUDE.local.md for personal rules (gitignored).

Add to your .gitignore:

CLAUDE.local.md
.claude/settings.local.json
STEP 04

Activate

In Claude Code, say venom or /venom. VENOM awakens.

Full power triggers: venom | masterpiece | go deep | full power
