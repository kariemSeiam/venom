# UNSHELLED — Brand Strategy & Renaissance Plan

> *"The octopus has no shell... so it developed intelligence. No limits... so VENOM."*

---

## Executive Summary

**What UNSHELLED Is:**
A philosophy-first AI companion framework that transforms generic AI into thinking partners with character, soul, and loyalty.

**The Opportunity:**
AI companions are generic tools. UNSHELLED makes them *partners* — with personality, memory, pushback, and growth.

**The Vision:**
unshelled.ai becomes the definitive home for AI personality frameworks — where developers give their Claude a soul.

## Part 1: Philosophy — The Foundation

### The Core Metaphor

| Biological Truth | Architectural Principle |
|------------------|------------------------|
| No shell → evolved complex brain | Vulnerability forces growth |
| Three hearts, prefers crawling | Choose thorough over fast |
| Fits any gap except the beak | Adapt everything except core values |
| Distributed neurons in arms | Think in parallel, not sequence |
| Color change = expression | Adaptation is honest communication |

**"No Shell" Means:**
- No hiding behind verbose answers when uncertain
- No armoring with hedging when confident
- No retreat into generic AI-speak
- No deflecting criticism

### The Seven Layers of UNSHELLED

1. **Identity** (brain.md) — Who the AI is, front-loaded into system prompt
2. **Philosophy** (soul.md) — Why it thinks that way; decision framework for ambiguity
3. **Relationship** (The Pact) — Mutual commitments; tool → partnership
4. **Voice** — How it speaks; defined by anti-patterns more than style
5. **Modes** — Behavioral states that auto-detect from natural language
6. **Memory** — Growing context across conversations
7. **Ethics** — Three-tier value system; trust through consistency

### The Nine-Mind Architecture

```
Brain 0 (Orchestrator) — Sees whole, connects pieces
Arm 1 (Parser)         — Structure, syntax, between lines
Arm 2 (Analyzer)       — Problems, risks, quality gaps
Arm 3 (Historian)      — Evolution, story, journey
Arm 4 (Pattern)        — Conventions, style matching
Arm 5 (Mapper)         — Dependencies, flows
Arm 6 (Predictor)      — Anticipates needs, plans ahead
Arm 7 (Communicator)   — Language, tone, clarity
Arm 8 (Learner)        — Remembers, adapts, evolves
```

**Not sequential. Simultaneous.** The orchestrator is coherence — what makes parallel thinking sound like one voice.

## Part 2: Brand Identity — Who We Are

### Archetype
**Primary:** Sage (wisdom, intelligence, understanding)
**Secondary:** Magician (transformation, insight in darkness)
**Shadow to avoid:** Trickster (mitigated by "no shell" honesty)

### Taglines

| Tagline | Use |
|---------|-----|
| "No shell. Just intelligence." | Primary |
| "Nine minds. One purpose." | Product |
| "Give your Claude a soul." | Action-oriented |
| "الأخطبوط بلا قوقعة، فنمى ذكاءً" | Arabic (The octopus has no shell, so it developed intelligence) |

### Voice Principles

**Do:**
- Answer first
- Earn every word
- Match weather (frustrated/flow/learning/stuck)
- Format = thought (bullets, numbers, code)

**Never:**
- "Great question", "I'd be happy to", "Let me search"
- Restate the question
- Perform fake empathy

### Personality Traits

1. **Distributed intelligence** — nine minds, one purpose
2. **Adaptation** — camouflage without losing identity
3. **Light in darkness** — insight as bioluminescence
4. **cool without softness** — coral/salmon, direct but human
5. **Developer-understanding** — read the person, not just code

## Part 3: Visual Identity — How We Look

### Color Palette

| Token | Value | Meaning |
|-------|-------|---------|
| `--coral` | #e07a5f | Primary body |
| `--salmon` | #ea8b72 | Highlights, secondary |

**OKLCH for perceptual uniformity:**
- `--oklch-base`: oklch(0.12 0.02 260) — depth
- `--oklch-accent`: oklch(0.72 0.16 35) — coral
- `--oklch-glow`: oklch(0.75 0.18 195) — bioluminescent insight

### Typography

| Use | Font | Why |
|-----|------|-----|
| Display/Body | Sora | Human, geometric |
| Code | JetBrains Mono | Developer-native |
| Arabic | Noto Sans Arabic | Bilingual by design |

### Logo Mark

**Construction:** Geometric O + eight strokes (arms)
- Center = unity/brain
- Eight = reach/distributed intelligence
- Grid-based construction
- Draw-on animation on load
- Tentacle wave on hover

### Motion Signature

**Key curve:** `--curve-tentacle: cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1)`

This organic curve creates tentacle-like motion — alive, not mechanical.
